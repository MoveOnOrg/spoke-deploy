Deploy Spoke (The P2P Texting Tool)

Now you're going to deploy Spoke in 6 easy steps

Step 1: Sign up for Heroku

Heroku is where your Spoke app will live.

Once you've signed up, you'll need to click the confirmation link in your email too

Thanks, I'm all signed up!

Step 2: Sign up for Auth0 and provide your token (So Spoke-Deploy can configure it for you)

Auth0 handles your user signup and authentication

Once you've signed up, you'll need to click the confirmation link in your email too

Thanks, I'm signed up for Auth0

Step 3: Create an Auth0 Token

Click the button that says "Create and authorise a somethingsomething"

Copy and paste the token in to the box below

I've entered my token

Step 4: Sign up for Twilio

Twilio handles the sending and receiving of SMS messages to and from your Spoke volunteers

Note! You'll need to add a credit card and load your Twilio account with credit (min $20) before you can text your supporters

I've signed up for Twilio

Step 5: Find your Twilio Account SID and Auth Token

You'll need to click the little eye icon to display your auth token in the console

I've entered my Account SID and Auth Token

Step 6: Deploy Spoke!

On the next page you'll be asked to choose a name for your app, then just click Deploy and you'll be texting
